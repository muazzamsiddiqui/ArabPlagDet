Corpus

Our corpus consists of 247 pairs of suspicious-source document pairs. The suspicious documents are assignments submitted by students in an Introduction to IT course. The corpus can be downloaded from here

Please cite the following paper if you use the corpus

Developing an Arabic Plagiarism Detection Corpus, MA Siddiqui, IH Khan, K Mansoor Jambi, S Omar Elhaj, A Bagais, Computer Science & Information Technology (CS & IT) 4 (2014), 261-269
